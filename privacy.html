Privacy Policy

Garage Book Pro ("we", "the app") is a vehicle expense tracking application. This page explains what information we collect, why, and how it is protected.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: your email address or phone number, used only for login/authentication (via Firebase Authentication).
  • Vehicle & expense data: vehicle names, registration numbers, odometer readings, expense entries, categories, and payment modes that you enter yourself.
  • Usage data: basic app interaction data needed for the app to function (e.g. which tier your account is on).

We do not collect location data, contacts, camera/microphone access, or any data beyond what you directly enter into the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate your account and sync your data across your devices.
  • To provide the features you use (reports, comparisons, backups).
  • To identify and fix bugs affecting the app's reliability.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or third parties. We do not run ads inside the app.

3. Where Your Data Is Stored

Your data is stored on Google Firebase (Realtime Database), a secure cloud infrastructure provider. Access to your data is restricted to your own logged-in account through security rules — no other user can read or modify your data.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account exists. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings → Account → Account Delete inside the app. This action is irreversible.

5. Premium Subscription

Garage Book Pro Premium is currently activated via a manually issued redeem code (not an automated payment gateway). No card or banking details are collected or stored by the app itself.

6. Children's Privacy

Garage Book Pro is intended for general vehicle-owning users and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
